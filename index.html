About Me

Precision, Passion,
and a Perfect Seam

I am a professional seamstress with a deep love for the craft — whether I'm transforming a beloved dress to fit like it was made for you, constructing a bespoke bag from scratch, or performing the delicate stitchwork required in cutting-edge medical research.

My background spans fashion alterations and custom garment construction to highly specialised work suturing artificial heart valves for a medical research start-up. Every project, no matter the scale, receives the same meticulous attention to detail and commitment to quality.

I am available for alterations, custom commissions, and collaborative creative projects. I would love to help bring your vision to life.
